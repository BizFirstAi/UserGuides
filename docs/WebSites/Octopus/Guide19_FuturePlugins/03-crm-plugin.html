Octopus — Planned

CRM Plugin (Planned)

The planned CRM plugin will connect Octopus agents to Salesforce and HubSpot — enabling sales agents to read contacts and opportunities, log calls, update deal stages, and create activities without leaving the chat interface.

Planned feature. This plugin is in the design phase. Details may change before release.

Planned MCP Tools

ToolDescription
crm_get_contactLook up a contact by email, phone, or name
crm_list_opportunitiesList open opportunities for a contact or account
crm_update_opportunityUpdate deal stage, close date, or amount on an opportunity
crm_log_activityLog a call, email, or meeting as a CRM activity
crm_create_contactCreate a new contact record
crm_searchFull-text search across contacts, accounts, and opportunities
