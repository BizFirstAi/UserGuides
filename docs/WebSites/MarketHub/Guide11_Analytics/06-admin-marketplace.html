Admin Marketplace Analytics

Admin marketplace analytics provide a platform-wide view of marketplace health — total install volumes, active publisher counts, category performance, top-10 package charts, and at-risk publisher identification. These metrics support operational oversight and strategic platform decisions.

Platform-Level Metrics

MetricDefinition
Total installs (period)Sum of all unique tenant installs across all packages in the selected date range
Total active tenantsCount of distinct tenants with at least one active package install
Active publishersPublishers who published or updated at least one package in the selected period
New packages publishedCount of first-ever package publications in the period
New certificationsCount of Certified certifications granted in the period
Total packagesAll-time count of published packages (active, deprecated, and hidden)

Category Health

The category health dashboard breaks down marketplace activity by package category. For each category:

Category MetricWhat It Shows
Installs in category (period)Total installs across all packages in the category — shows which categories are in high demand
Average review score in categoryMean of all review scores in the category — identifies quality gaps or standout categories
New packages in category (period)Publisher activity in the category — high new package counts indicate publisher interest in the space
Active publishers in categoryNumber of publishers with at least one package in the category
Category growth ratePeriod-over-period install growth for the category

Top 10 Charts

The admin analytics dashboard includes several Top 10 charts, all scoped to the selected date range:

Top 10 Packages by Installs

The ten packages with the most new installs in the period. Used to identify dominant packages and emerging breakouts.

Top 10 Packages by Review Score

Highest-rated packages with a minimum review count threshold (10+ reviews) to filter out packages with too few reviews to be meaningful.

Top 10 Publishers by Points Earned

Publishers who earned the most reputation points in the period — showing who is most actively growing their marketplace presence.

Top 10 Categories by Growth

Categories with the highest install growth rate period-over-period — surfaces emerging demand areas for platform development focus.

At-Risk Publishers

The at-risk publishers panel automatically flags publishers meeting any of these criteria:

Risk SignalCriteriaRecommended Action
Declining installsInstall count declined more than 30% month-over-month for 2 consecutive monthsReview publisher's packages for documentation gaps or competitive displacement; consider outreach
Critical issue backlogPublisher has 3+ open Critical issues unresolved for more than 30 daysMay trigger certification review; flag for admin follow-up
Low rating trendPublisher's average rating dropped below 3.0 in the last 30 days (minimum 5 reviews)Investigate review content for patterns; consider quality consultation outreach
Inactive publisherNo package updates or community activity in more than 90 days with active installsVerify publisher is still maintaining their packages; reach out if contact info is available
At-risk flags are advisory, not automatic actions

The at-risk publisher panel is an admin attention tool — it surfaces patterns for human review. No automatic action (demotions, notifications to publishers, or certification reviews) is triggered by an at-risk flag alone. Admins review flags and decide whether to take action based on the full context.
