Official Certification

Official is the highest certification level on MarketHub — reserved exclusively for packages authored or endorsed by BizFirstGO and its approved integration partners. It cannot be applied for through the standard certification process.

What Official Means

An Official badge signals the highest possible level of trust and endorsement on the marketplace. It means BizFirstGO has either:

  • Authored the package directly as a reference implementation or platform template, or
  • Formally reviewed and endorsed the package as part of an approved integration partner program

Official vs Certified Comparison

AspectCertifiedOfficial
Who can earn itAny publisher who meets the criteriaBizFirstGO team and approved partners only

Official Package Examples

Official packages appear in every product category — they are the authoritative reference implementations published by BizFirstGO itself:

  • Workflows — BizFirstGO reference approval workflows, onboarding process templates
  • Forms — Standard form definitions used in BizFirstGO platform examples
  • Node Libraries — Core execution node collections maintained by the platform team
  • Apps — Official BizFirstGO App Studio application templates
For aspiring integration partners

If you are building a deep integration with the BizFirstGO platform and want to pursue Official certification for your packages, contact the BizFirstGO partner team through the official partner program inquiry page. Partner relationships are evaluated individually and include a commercial agreement alongside the technical review process.
