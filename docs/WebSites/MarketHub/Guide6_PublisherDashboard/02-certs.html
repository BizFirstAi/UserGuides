My Certifications

The certifications section of the publisher workspace shows earned certifications, in-progress applications, and the criteria available to pursue — giving publishers a clear roadmap to increase their package trust level.

Certification Status Sections

SectionWhat It Shows
Earned CertificationsPackages that have achieved Certified status — with the date granted
In ProgressCertification applications submitted but not yet reviewed — with estimated review completion
Available to ApplyPublished packages that meet the minimum criteria and are eligible for Certified application
Not EligiblePublished packages that do not yet meet criteria — with a checklist showing what's missing

Certification Criteria Checklist

For each package in "Available to Apply" or "Not Eligible", the workspace shows a checklist of the Certified criteria:

CriterionStatus Display
README / documentation URLProvided or Missing
Test coverage report (80%+)Coverage percentage with Passes or Below 80%
Security scan — no Critical/High findingsLast scan date with Clean or Findings
Open critical issuesCount of open critical issues — must be 0
Changelog completenessComplete or Incomplete

Applying for Certified Status

1

Verify All Criteria Are Met

Confirm all checklist items show green before applying. Submitting with failing criteria will result in rejection and waste the review cycle.

2

Submit Evidence

Upload the test coverage report (PDF or HTML) and provide the documentation URL. These are reviewed by the admin.

3

Await Review

Estimated 3–5 business days. The package remains published in Community tier while the application is under review.

4

Receive Decision

Approved: Certified badge applied immediately, 100 reputation points credited. Rejected: feedback provided explaining which criteria were not met. Publisher can address and reapply.
