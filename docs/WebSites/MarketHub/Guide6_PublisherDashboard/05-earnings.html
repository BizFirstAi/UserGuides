MarketHub

Earnings

The earnings section is available when marketplace monetization is enabled — showing revenue from paid packages, payout history, pending balance, and bank configuration for receiving payments.

Marketplace monetization is instance-configurable

The Earnings section is only available if the platform operator has enabled marketplace monetization on this MarketHub instance. On community and self-hosted instances, this section may show a "Monetization not enabled" message.

Revenue by Package

The earnings page shows a breakdown of revenue by individual package — useful for understanding which packages are generating revenue and for pricing strategy decisions. Each package row shows:

  • Package name and pricing model (one-time / subscription)
  • Price set by publisher
  • Active installs (paying customers)
  • Revenue this month and all-time
  • Marketplace fee (30%) and publisher net (70%)

Payout History

The payout history table shows all completed payouts — date, amount, and bank reference number. Payouts are processed monthly on the 15th for all balances over the minimum payout threshold ($50 USD or equivalent).
