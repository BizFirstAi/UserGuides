Rewards Model

MarketHub rewards publishers for real marketplace impact. Points accumulate from installs, reviews, certifications, and community activity — driving tier progression from Bronze through Platinum.

Points at a Glance

Tier Progression

Points accumulate into tier levels. Once a tier is reached, the publisher stays at that tier permanently — there is no demotion, even as reputation decay reduces the point total slightly over time.

Gold5,000 – 19,999GoldFeatured placement eligibility, Gold badge on profile and packages
Platinum20,000+PlatinumPriority search ranking boost, dedicated account support, Platinum badge

Reputation Decay

To keep the leaderboard reflective of current marketplace health (not just historical volume), installs and reviews older than two years contribute at 50% weight to the total point calculation. This means publishers need to keep publishing and engaging to maintain their position — but their tier is protected from decay.

Decay affects ranking, not tier

If a publisher's weighted total drops below a tier threshold due to decay, they retain their tier badge. Decay only affects their position in the leaderboard rankings and their actual point total display.

Admin Rewards

Administrators can manually award points and badges to publishers for exceptional contributions that fall outside the automated point system — such as exceptional bug reports, conference presentations, or significant community leadership.

All admin awards appear in the publisher's reputation history with the label "Admin Award" and include the admin's reason text. Awards above 500 points require dual-admin approval before being credited.
